Project Statement

The project aims to develop a comprehensive Car Rental Service application that provides a user-friendly platform for seamless car rentals from multiple locations, automating the rental process and ensuring efficient management by employees for enhanced customer satisfaction.

Requirements Specification

The database for E-Z Car Rental will store comprehensive information about rental cars, locations, customers, and transactions, facilitating seamless car rental services. Users can search for cars by zip code, city, or address and filter by features such as make, model, color, and transmission. Each user and car will have unique IDs to track reservations, conditions, and transaction histories. The system will ensure unique user credentials, verify legal rental requirements, and update car statuses from "available" to "rented" and back. Maintenance records, including service and repair histories, will be meticulously tracked. The transaction table will manage rental periods, payments, and any additional charges or refunds due to damages or cancellations. This database will also support business analysis to identify popular car types, profitable dates, and locations for targeted marketing efforts.

Queries Example

Finding incomplete transaction details with user information for a specific transaction ID for employee management

				
SELECT T.transactionID, U.firstName, U.lastName, U.phoneNo 
FROM Transaction T JOIN Users U on T.userID = U.userID 
WHERE T.status = 'INCOMPLETE' 
ORDER BY T.transactionID;
