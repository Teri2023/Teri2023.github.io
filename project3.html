DREXEL ZOO

Drexel Zoo's mobile application offers a thrilling gateway to a world of extraordinary wonders and limitless entertainment. Members of Drexel Zoo can enjoy exclusive app-only perks and discounts, providing access to a host of additional benefits that enhance every visit. These benefits include fast-track e-ticket entry, members-only events, prime-time visiting analytics, and special animal encounters. Being a part of the Drexel Zoo community means enjoying a unique experience that cannot be found anywhere else. The excitement extends to dining experiences as well. You can use our application to book animal-themed cuisine at designated eateries within the zoo premises. Every meal is a celebration of the rich and fascinating wildlife world, from the majestic roar of lions to the playful antics of monkeys. Drexel Zoo ensures that every moment is a new discovery waiting to happen.
